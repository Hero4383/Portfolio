How it works

Bank transfers are available only for contracted work. Please use the contract ID so payments can be matched to the correct agreement.

  • Confirm you have an active contract and a contract ID.
  • Send a request for transfer details using the template below.
  • Receive ACH or wire instructions after verification.
  • Send payment from your bank and include the contract ID in the memo.

Fees depend on your bank and transfer type. ACH is often low-fee within the USA.
